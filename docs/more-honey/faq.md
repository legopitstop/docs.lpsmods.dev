# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `More Honey`?

**More Honey** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds over five new honey-related foods to make honey more useful for survival and buffs. It expands the food system with items like honey sandwiches and honey pancakes that restore health/hunger and can remove poison, enhancing gameplay around honey and food.
