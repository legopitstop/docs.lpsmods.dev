# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Canned`?

**Canned** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds blocks for storing food items in "cans" letting you stack and decorate with up to eight food cans per block. It also includes a can opener that returns the food items and an empty can when used, making food storage neat and functional.
