# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Record API`?

**Record API** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds an easy system for Minecraft to supports tons of custom music discs without replacing the vanilla ones. It gives creators a data-driven way to add up to thousands of new discs, including a way to obtain them (like from mobs), along with an example pack to build your own custom music content.
