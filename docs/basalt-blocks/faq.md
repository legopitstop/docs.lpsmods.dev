# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Basalt Blocks`?

**Basalt Blocks** is a Minecraft mod and Add-On that adds lots of new basalt-based building blocks and variants to expand the limited basalt options in vanilla minecraft. It includes slab, stairs, walls, buttons, pressure plates, polished and cobbled basalt variants, and more to give builders more decorative and functional basalt assets.
