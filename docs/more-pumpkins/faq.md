# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `More Pumpkins`?

**More Pumpkins** is a Minecraft mod, datapack, and Add-On that adds hundreds of new carved pumpkin and jack o' lantern variants to your world for more decoration options and creative designs. It includes varied patterns and a special Soul Jack O' Lantern with a blue glow, expanding the vanilla pumpkin building choices.
