# How to Enable Content Log GUI

Step-by-step instructions on how to enable the content log GUI in [Minecraft: Bedrock Edition](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Bedrock_Edition)

## Universal

1. Open Minecraft: Bedrock Edition
2. Press "Settings".
   ![step2](https://cdn.lpsmods.dev/data/docs/df54e058-c865-4dd0-b561-d5bca1bfbc54.webp)

3. At the bottom press "Creator".
   ![step3](https://cdn.lpsmods.dev/data/docs/57e596d2-2edb-411b-981f-c444969582df.webp)

4. Press "Enable Content Log GUI"
   ![step4](https://cdn.lpsmods.dev/data/docs/b74ef86e-48ac-49e1-994c-1a8a7a412e17.webp)

5. Now, Whenever a pack has errors it will show it at the top of your screen.
