# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Spawner Craft`?

**Spawner Craft** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds craftable spawn eggs and mob spawner crafting recipes to your world, letting you customize and control which mob a spawner will produce. It lets you craft any spawn egg and use it on a placed spawner to make it spawn that mob using in-game recipes rather than commands or creative mode.

## How do I craft the custom spawn eggs?

In v1.12.0 the custom spawn eggs have been removed since Mojang has finally added the Iron and Snow Golem Spawn Eggs.

## How do I craft a Wither or Ender Dragon Spawn Egg?

Since these spawn eggs are only obtainable using commands I figured it would be unsafe to add them in as crafting recipes and I removed them from the custom drop crafting.

## My Spawner is not spawning any mobs even through I can see the mob spinning inside?

To spawn a certain mob it needs to match a few conditions that are different for most mobs. For example; a zombie spawner needs to be dark (light level 0) while for pigs you need to have grass around the spawner or water for a fish to spawn. You can probably Google and figure out which conditions are needed to spawn said mob.
