# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Barked`?

**Barked** is a Minecraft mod, datapack, and Add-On that adds a bark item drop when you strip logs, wood, stems, or hyphae in your world. It lets you collect bark, use it as a basic fuel, and reapply it back onto stripped blocks to restore their original appearance.
