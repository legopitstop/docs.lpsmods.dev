# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Lot's More Food`?

**Lot's More Food** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds over 200 new food items, crops and edible content to your world for more variety in survival and crafting. It lets you grow, craft, and eat many more foods than vanilla offers, expanding the culinary and farming experience.

## How to craft the food?

:::tabs key:loader

== Add-On

Use the regular crafting table to craft all the items.

== Mod

Use the regular crafting table to craft all the items.

== Datapack

You'll need to use a custom crafting table.

Obtainable by renaming a [Crafting Table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Crafting_Table) to `Custom Crafting Table`

:::

## How to get the fruits/veggies?

:::tabs key:loader

== Add-On

Crop, bushes, and trees generate around your world.

== Mod

Right now plants will not generate around the world yet.

== Datapack

Right now plants will not generate around the world yet.

:::
