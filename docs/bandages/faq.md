# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `Bandages`?

**Bandages** is a Minecraft mod, datapack, and Add-On that adds healing items called bandages to the game, letting you recover health and hunger quickly in survival. It introduces several types of bandages (overworld, nether, end, and water variants) with extra effects like fire resistance or water breathing when used.
