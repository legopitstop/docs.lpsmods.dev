# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `More Gold`?

**More Gold** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds over five new gold themed foods and golden items to help you restore health and hunger with useful buffs. It includes golden fruits, enchanted golden foods, and special golden blocks/wood types, expanding crafting and survival options.
