# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions you might have.

## What is `More Blocks`?

**More Blocks** is a Minecraft mod, datapack and Add-On that adds new variants of blocks like stairs, slabs, vertical slabs, and layer blocks to expand building options. It enhances creative and decorative building by giving you more structural block types, though it requires the Assets+ pack for full support.
